Muscles of Mastication

Muscles of Mastication
Muscle Origin Insertion Action Nerve Supply Main Function
Primary Muscles Masseter Zygomatic arch Angle and ramus of mandible Elevation of the mandible (closing the jaw) Trigeminal nerve (V3) - Masseteric branch Chewing, biting
Temporalis Temporal fossa and temporal surface of the skull Coronoid process of the mandible Elevation and retraction of the mandible Trigeminal nerve (V3) - Deep temporal branches Chewing, biting, and closing the jaw
Medial Pterygoid Medial surface of the lateral pterygoid plate and tuberosity of the maxilla Medial surface of the angle of the mandible Elevation of the mandible and lateral deviation (grinding) Trigeminal nerve (V3) - Medial pterygoid branch Chewing and grinding
Lateral Pterygoid Lateral surface of the lateral pterygoid plate and greater wing of sphenoid bone Articular disc and neck of the mandible (condyle) Protrusion of the mandible, depression, and lateral deviation Trigeminal nerve (V3) - Lateral pterygoid branch Assists in grinding and lateral jaw movement
Secondary Muscles Buccinator Alveolar processes of maxilla and mandible, pterygomandibular raphe Orbicularis oris at the corner of the mouth Compresses cheeks (whistling, sucking) Facial nerve (CN VII) Blowing, whistling
Suprahyoid Muscles Digastric Anterior belly: Digastric fossa of mandible; Posterior belly: Mastoid notch of temporal bone Intermediate tendon connected to hyoid bone Elevates hyoid bone and depresses mandible (opening mouth) Anterior belly: Trigeminal nerve (V3) - Mylohyoid branch; Posterior belly: Facial nerve (CN VII) Chewing, swallowing
Mylohyoid Mandible (mylohyoid line) Hyoid bone and median raphe Elevates hyoid bone and floor of mouth (assists in swallowing) Trigeminal nerve (V3) - Mylohyoid branch Swallowing, speaking
Geniohyoid Mental spine of mandible Hyoid bone Elevates hyoid bone and depresses mandible (pulls it forward) Cervical branch of hypoglossal nerve (CN XII) Swallowing, speaking
Infrahyoid Muscles Sternohyoid Sternum and medial end of clavicle Hyoid bone Depresses hyoid bone after swallowing Anterior rami of cervical spinal nerves (C1-C3) Swallowing, speaking
Sternothyroid Sternum Thyroid cartilage Depresses thyroid cartilage Anterior rami of cervical spinal nerves (C1-C3) Speaking
Thyrohyoid Thyroid cartilage Hyoid bone Depresses hyoid or elevates larynx if hyoid is fixed Anterior rami of cervical spinal nerves (C1-C3) Speaking
Omohyoid Superior border of scapula Hyoid bone Depresses and retracts hyoid bone Ansa cervicalis (C1-C3) Swallowing, speaking

Zygomaticus Minor Zygomatic bone Upper lip Facial nerve (CN VII) Facial artery Raises the upper lip (assists in smiling)
Levator Labii Superioris Maxilla and zygomatic bone Skin and muscle of the upper lip Facial nerve (CN VII) Facial artery Elevates the upper lip (snarling)
Levator Labii Superioris Alaeque Nasi Maxilla and frontal process of maxilla Skin of the upper lip and ala of the nose Facial nerve (CN VII) Facial artery Elevates the upper lip and dilates the nostrils (flaring the nostrils)
Depressor Labii Inferioris Mandible Lower lip Facial nerve (CN VII) Facial artery Pulls down the lower lip (pouting)
Mentalis Mandible Chin and skin of the lower lip Facial nerve (CN VII) Facial artery Elevates and protrudes the lower lip (pouting)
Platysma Fascia of chest and deltoid muscle Mandible and skin of the lower face Cervical branch of facial nerve (CN VII) Submental artery Tenses the neck, grimace
