TU MBBS Syllabus - 1st Year

Paper-I: Community Medicine I


General Concept

The course is designed to teach basic knowledge and skills to diagnose and treat common medical conditions in various systems of the body. The course includes principles of history taking, physical examination, and management of common illnesses.

Musculo-skeletal and Nervous System

The course is designed to teach basic knowledge and skills to diagnose and manage common musculoskeletal and nervous system disorders. The course includes principles of history taking, physical examination, and management of common illnesses.

Community Medicine

The course is designed to teach basic knowledge and skills to understand the principles of community health and to diagnose and treat common public health problems. The course includes the principles of epidemiology, biostatistics, environmental health, and health promotion.
