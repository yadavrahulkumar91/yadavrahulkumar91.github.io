Mid-brain syndromes

Weber's syndrome

  • Occlusion of the branches of posterior cerebral artery that supplies midbrain
  • Necrosis of the tissue involving CN III and Crus cerebri.

Benedict's syndrome

  • Similar to the Weber's syndrome but necrosis involves Medial lemniscus and thesiss nuclesus

Perinaud's syndrome

  • Tumor of Pineal gland suppresses the Superior Colliculus nucleus
