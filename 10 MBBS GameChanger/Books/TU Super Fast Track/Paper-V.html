Present Tense Type Structure Sentences Nepali Translation
Present Simple Subject + V1 + O She writes poetry. उहाँ कविता लेख्छिन्।
Present Continuous Subject + am/is/are + V4 + O They are playing football. उनीहरू फुटबल खेल्दैछन्।
Present Perfect Subject + have/has + V3 + O They have finished their homework. उनीहरूले आफ्नो होमवर्क सम्पन्न गरेका छन्।
Present Perfect Continuous Subject + have/has + been + V4 + O They have been studying for hours. उनीहरू घण्टेहरूदेखि पढ्दैछन्।
Past Tense Type Structure Sentences Nepali Translation
Past Simple Subject + V2 + O I played basketball yesterday. म भोली बास्केटबल खेलें।
Past Continuous Subject + was/were + V4 + O He was studying when the phone rang. उहाँले फोन बजेको बेला अध्ययन गर्दै थिए।
Past Perfect Subject + had + V3 + O She had finished the book before the movie started. उहाँले फिल्म सुरु भएको अघि उहाँले पुस्तक सम्पन्न गरिसकेकी थिइन्।
Past Perfect Continuous Subject + had + been + V4 + O We had been waiting for hours before the concert began. हामीले संगीतको संध्या सुरु भएको अघि घण्टेहरूदेखि प्रतीक्षा गरिसकेका थिए।
Future Tense Type Structure Sentences Nepali Translation
Simple Future Subject + will/shall + V1 + O I will visit my grandparents tomorrow. म भोलि मेरा बाजेबाजुलाई भेट्ने छु।
Future Continuous Subject + will/shall + be + V4 + O She will be studying all evening. उहाँले सारा दिन अध्ययन गर्नेछ।
Future Perfect Subject + will/shall + have + V3 + O They will have finished their project by next week. उनीहरू अर्को हप्तामा आफ्नो प्रोजेक्ट सम्पन्न गरेका हुनेछन्।
Future Perfect Continuous Subject + will/shall + have + been + V4 + O He will have been working for 10 hours by the time he finishes. उहाँ सम्पन्न भएपछि १० घण्टा भन्दा धेरै काम गरेको हुनेछ।
