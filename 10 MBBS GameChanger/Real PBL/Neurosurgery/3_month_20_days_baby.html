About myelomeningocele

  1. Extension of CNS tissue through defect in the vertebral column.
  2. Also known as Meningomyelocele
  3. Meningocele$\rightarrow$Extrusion of only Meninges
  4. Meningomyelocele$\rightarrow$Extrusion of Meninges along with Spinal cord
  5. Common site of occurrence$\rightarrow$ Lumbosacral region
  6. Commonly complicated with $\rightarrow$ Superimposed infection that extends into the cord from the skin

Symptoms

  1. Motor and Sensory deficits in the lower extremities.
  2. Disturbances in bowel and bladder control.
