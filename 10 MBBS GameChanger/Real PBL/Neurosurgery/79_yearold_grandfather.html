History

My male patient of age 79 years presented with sudden unconsciousness for few days. He has been suffering from sudden recurrence of unconsciousness for last 2-3 years. He is also a diabetes patient. He belongs from inner terai of Nepal.

About Hydrocephalus

Hydrocephalus is the accumulation of excess Cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) within brain ventricles.

Normal circulation of CSF

Production of CSF by Choroid plexus(a network of blood vessels in each ventricle of the brain) $\rightarrow$ Circulation of CSF into each ventricles $\rightarrow$ Absorbed by Arachnoid granulations.

Common cause of Hydrocephalus

  1. Impaired flow
  2. Impaired resorption of CSF

Symptoms of myelomeningocele

  1. Motor and Sensory deficits in the lower extremities.
  2. Disturbances in bowel and bladder control.
