Gymnosperms

Gymnosperms are the botanical equivalent of a time capsule, containing living relics from the ancient forests of the past.

Gymnosperm

Gymnosperm is a naked seeded plant because ovary is absent.

Some members of gymnosperm are called living fossils:

  • Cycas
  • Ginkgo
  • Metasequoia

Living fossil is such type of organism which is present in both living and fossil form but they have no evolutionary changes.

Pollination is anemophilous type (by wind).

Pollination of gymnosperm is defined as transfer of microspore from microsporophyll to ovule directly.

Ovule is orthotropous except Pinus → Anatropous. (PAN)
