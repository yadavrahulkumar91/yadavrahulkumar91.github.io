Plant Stages

  1. In the life cycle of plant, 2 types of stage is present i.e.,
    • Sporophytic (Spore producing stage),
    • Gametophytic (Gamete producing stage)
  2. In thallophyte and Bryophyte, main plant body is haploid and gametophytic.
  3. In Pteridophyta, main plant body is diploid and sporophytic.
    • In pteridophyte, both sporophyte and gametophyte are independent. So distinct alternation of generation is found.
      1. Alternation of Generation
        • When multicellular diploid sporophytic phase alternate with multicellular gametophytic stage and vice-versa, is called Alternation of Generation.
        • Example: Embryophtes (From Bryophytes to Pteridopytes)
        • Alternation of Generation present in:
          1. Bryophyta
          2. Pteridophyta
          3. Gymnosperm
          4. All
        • Distinct alternation of Generation is found in:
          1. Bryophyta
          2. Pteridophyta
          3. Gymnosperm
          4. Angiosperm
  4. In Gymnosperms and Angiosperm, main plant body is diploid and sporophytic.
