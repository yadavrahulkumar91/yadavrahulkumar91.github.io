A. Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is simply defined as the formation of carbohydrates from \(CO_2\) and \(H_2O\) by illuminated green cells of plants.

B. Reaction

\(6CO_2 + 12H_2O^{18} \xrightarrow{{\text{Light}}} C_6H_{12}O_6 + 6O_2^{18‚Üë} + 6H_2O\)

  1. \(CO_2\) and \(H_2O\) ‚Üí Raw material

  2. \(C_6H_{12}O_6\) ‚Üí Required product

  3. \(O_2\) and \(H_2O\) ‚Üí Byproduct

  4. Main/Ultimate source of energy is sunlight.

    1. Maximum photosynthesis occurs in white light/sunlight.
    2. Best spectrum for photosynthesis (Effective) = White light > Red > Blue > Green
    3. Best absorption spectrum for photosynthesis = White light > Blue > Red > Green

  5. \(O_2\) evolved during photosynthesis comes from \(H_2O\) and not from \(CO_2\).

    This was experimentally confirmed by Ruben and Kamen while working on Chlorella alga (unicellular green alga) using radioactive isotopes oxygen, i.e., \(O_2^{18}\).

  6. \(O_2\) present in glucose molecule and water comes from \(CO_2\).

  7. About 90% of total photosynthesis in the world is done by algae (mainly by BGA) in oceans and freshwater. [IOM 2010]

    Volume of \(CO_2\) absorbed in photosynthesis is almost equal to \(O_2\) liberated. [IOM 2006]
