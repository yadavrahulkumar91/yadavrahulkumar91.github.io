1. 1. In Pteridium circinate-vernation is found in:
    1. (a) Rhizome
    2. (b) Rachis
    3. (c) Fruit
    4. (d) Leaf
    (d) In Pteridium, circinate vernation is found in the leaves.
  2. 2. Which structure protects a group of sporangium in a fern:
    1. (a) Indusium
    2. (b) Perichaetium
    3. (c) Annulus
    4. (d) Stomium
    (a) The structure that protects a group of sporangia in a fern is called indusium.
  3. 3. Gametophyte of fern is:
    1. (a) Independent body
    2. (b) Parasite on sporophyte
    3. (c) Partial parasite on sporophyte
    4. (d) None
    (a) The gametophyte of a fern is an independent body.
  4. 4. Male gamete in fern is:
    1. (a) Uniflagellated
    2. (b) Biflagellated
    3. (c) Multiflagellated
    4. (d) None
    (b) Male gamete in a fern is biflagellated.
  5. 5. Frond is the name of leaves of:
    1. (a) Ferns
    2. (b) Moss
    3. (c) Conifer
    4. (d) Cycas
    (a) Frond is the name of leaves of ferns.
  6. 6. The vascular cryptogam is:
    1. (a) Bryophytes
    2. (b) Thallophytes
    3. (c) Pteridophytes
    4. (d) Fungi
    (c) The vascular cryptogam refers to pteridophytes.
  7. 7. Sporangia in fern are found in:
    1. (a) Rhizome
    2. (b) Leaves
    3. (c) Stem
    4. (d) Roots
    (b) Sporangia in ferns are found in the leaves.
  8. 8. Stele in fern is:
    1. (a) Protostele
    2. (b) Siphonostele
    3. (c) Voleastele
    4. (d) Dictyostele
    (b) The stele in fern is known as siphonostele.
  9. 9. A plant which has vascular tissue but lacks seeds is placed in:
    1. (a) Thallophyte
    2. (b) Bryophyte
    3. (c) Pteridophyte
    4. (d) Gymnosperm
    (c) A plant that has vascular tissue but lacks seeds is placed in the category of pteridophytes.
  10. 10. Another name for alternation of generation is:
    1. (a) Metagenesis
    2. (b) Metamorphosis
    3. (c) Metanotum
    4. (d) None
    (a) Another name for alternation of generation is metagenesis.
  11. 11. Which is not true about alternation of generation?
    1. There are two phases in the life cycle
    2. Spores give rise to gametophyte
    3. Spores behave as gametes
    4. 1st diploid cell is zygote
    (c) Spores behaving as gametes is not true about alternation of generation.
  12. 12. Stele lacking pith is called:
    1. Protostele
    2. Dictyostele
    3. Siphonostele
    4. Meristele
    (b) Stele lacking pith is called dictyostele.
  13. 13. Which of the following is called Resurrection plant?
    1. Selaginella lepidophylla
    2. Salvinia
    3. Dryopteris filix-mas
    4. Adder's tongue
    (a) Selaginella lepidophylla is called the Resurrection plant.
  14. 14. Sporangium developed from a group of cells is called:
    1. Heterosporangiate development
    2. Leptosporangiate development
    3. Eusporangiate development
    4. Homosporangiate development
    (d) Sporangium developed from a group of cells is called homosporangiate development.
  15. 15. Ferns differ from moss in having:
    1. Independent sporophyte
    2. Independent gametophyte
    3. Archegonia
    4. Prothallus
    (a) Ferns differ from moss in having an independent sporophyte.
  16. 16. In Selaginella, ligule is found at:
    1. Dorsal side of microsporophyll
    2. Dorsal and ventral side of sporophyll
    3. Ventral side of sporophyll
    4. The base of each leaf
    (c) In Selaginella, the ligule is found on the ventral side of sporophyll.
  17. 17. Pteridophytes differ from bryophytes in respect of:
    1. Antherozoids
    2. Archegonium
    3. Conducting tissue
    4. None
    (c) Pteridophytes differ from bryophytes in respect of conducting tissue.
  18. 18. Pteridophytes differ from bryophytes in possessing:
    1. Alternation of generation
    2. Archegonia
    3. Vascular tissues
    4. Spores
    (c) Pteridophytes differ from bryophytes in possessing vascular tissues.
  19. 19. Vascular cryptogams is:
    1. Pteridophyta
    2. Bryophyta
    3. Algae
    4. Fungi
    (a) Vascular cryptogams refer to Pteridophyta.
  20. 20. In fern, which of the following cell is binucleate?
    1. Gametes
    2. Neck canal cell
    3. Venter canal cell
    4. Spore mother cell
    (b) In fern, the neck canal cell is binucleate.
  21. Which one of the following groups of plants shows independent alternation of generation?
    1. (a) Bryophyta
    2. (b) Pteridophyta
    3. (c) Both a and b
    4. (d) None of the above
    (c) Both Bryophyta and Pteridophyta show independent alternation of generation.
  22. Which group is called vascular cryptogam?
    1. (a) Thallophyta
    2. (b) Bryophyte
    3. (c) Pteridophyta
    4. (d) Gymnosperm
    (c) Pteridophyta is called vascular cryptogam.
  23. Apogamous sporophytes formed in ferns are:
    1. (a) Haploid
    2. (b) Diploid
    3. (c) Tetraploid
    4. (d) Triploid
    (b) Apogamous sporophytes formed in ferns are diploid.
  24. Gametangia of fern are produced on the:
    1. (a) Petiole
    2. (b) Rhizome
    3. (c) Leaves
    4. (d) Prothallus
    (d) Gametangia of fern are produced on the prothallus.
  25. Antherozoids of Pteridum are:
    1. (a) Aflagellated
    2. (b) Biflagellated
    3. (c) Multiflagellated
    4. (d) Quadriflagellated
    (b) Antherozoids of Pteridum are biflagellated.
