1. Lichens are ecologically important because:
    1. (a) They are primitive inhabitants of rock
    2. (b) They are association of algae and fungi
    3. (c) Can grow in SO2 polluted area
    4. (d) All of the above
    (d) Lichens are ecologically important because they are primitive inhabitants of rock, they are an association of algae and fungi, and they can grow in SO2 polluted areas.
  2. The mode of life of lichen is:
    1. (a) Obligatory parasite
    2. (b) Saprophyte
    3. (c) Symbiont
    4. (d) None
    (c) The mode of life of lichen is symbiont, as it is an association between algae and fungi.
  3. Which of the following is a composite dual organism?
    1. (a) Lichen
    2. (b) Algae
    3. (c) Fungi
    4. (d) Viruses
    (a) Lichen is a composite dual organism, consisting of an association between algae and fungi.
  4. Usnea is a:
    1. (a) Crustose lichen
    2. (b) Fructicose lichen
    3. (c) Foliose lichen
    4. (d) Fungi
    (b) Usnea is a fructicose lichen.
  5. Which is wrong about lichens?
    1. (a) They reproduce by fragmentation
    2. (b) Early inhabitants of rocks
    3. (c) Help in soil formation
    4. (d) They live in basic areas
    (d) The statement "They live in basic areas" is wrong about lichens.
  6. Organisms which are indicators of SO2 pollution in the air are:
    1. (a) Mosses
    2. (b) Lichens
    3. (c) Mushrooms
    4. (d) Puffballs
    (b) Lichens are indicators of SO2 pollution in the air.
  7. Lichens are indicators of:
    1. (a) Soil pollution
    2. (b) Water pollution
    3. (c) Sound pollution
    4. (d) Air pollution
    (d) Lichens are indicators of air pollution.
  8. Which one of the following is a fructose lichen?
    1. (a) Graphis
    2. (b) Parmelia
    3. (c) Cladonia
    4. (d) Rhizocarpon
    (c) Cladonia is a fructose lichen.
  9. Lichen is an association of:
    1. (a) Green algae and fungi
    2. (b) Blue-green algae and fungi
    3. (c) Red algae and fungi
    4. (d) Brown algae and fungi
    (a) Lichen is an association of green algae and fungi.
