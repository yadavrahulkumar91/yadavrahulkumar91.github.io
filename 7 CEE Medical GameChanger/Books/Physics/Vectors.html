Grade 11 Vector Lesson

A. Vectors and Scalars

a. Scalars:

  1. All potentials
  2. All fluxes
  3. Intensity of energy ⚡

b. Vectors

i. Examples

  1. All flux densities
  2. Gradients of all quantities
  3. All field strengths/intensities
  4. All dipole moments

ii. Special types of Vectors

  1. Polar vector
    • Vectors associated with linear directional effect.
      1. Displacement (s)
      2. Velocity (v)
      3. Acceleration (a)
      4. Momentum (P)
      5. Force (F)
  2. Axial vector
    • Vectors associated with rotation about an axis.
      1. Angular displacement (θ)
      2. Angular velocity (ω)
      3. Angular acceleration (α)
      4. Angular momentum (L/J)
      5. Moment of force/Torque (τ)

c. Tensor

- Quantity whose magnitude differs from direction to direction

  1. Pressure
  2. Stress
  3. Modulus of elasticity
  4. Moment of Inertia
  5. Coefficient of viscosity
