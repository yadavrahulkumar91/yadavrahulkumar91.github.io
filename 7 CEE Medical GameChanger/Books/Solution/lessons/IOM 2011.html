5 minutes
Less than 5 minute
More than 5 minutes
Less than or more than 5 minutes depending on whether its mass is more than or less than 1 kg

Therefore, we have: (65 - Ts) = k1 t1, and (60 - Ts) = k2 t2, where Ts is the temperature of the surroundings and k1 and k2 are constants of proportionality. Dividing the two equations, we get: t1/t2 = (65 - Ts)/(60 - Ts) Substituting the given values, we get: t1/t2 = 5/4 Therefore, t2 = (4/5) × t1 Since t1 is given as 5 minutes, t2 will be: t2 = (4/5) × 5 minutes = 4 minutes. Thus, the correct answer is (b) less than 5 minutes.
