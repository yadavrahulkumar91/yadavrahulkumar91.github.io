    2. (Cr =52, K=39)

    K2Cr2O7 + 3H2SO4 + 5(COOH) → 2K2SO4 + Cr2 {SO4)3 + 10CO2 + 8H2O

    1. 49
    2. 294
    3. 98
    4. 108
    (c) Equivalent weight of K2Cr2O7 can be calculated as its molecular weight divided by its valency factor. The valency factor of K2Cr2O7 is 6, which is the number of electrons that can be lost by the salt in the reaction. So, the equivalent weight of K2Cr2O7 is 294/6 = 49.
