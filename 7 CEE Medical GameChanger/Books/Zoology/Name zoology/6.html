1. 1. The food which Hydra can't digest is
    1. Fat

    1. Muscular cells
    2. Sensory cell
    3. Nematoblasts

    1. Annelida
    2. Coelenterata

    1. Compensatory hypertrophy
    2. Morphallaxis

    1. Porifera

  18. Why is self-fertilization absent in Hydra?
    1. (a) Monoecious
    2. (b) Hermaphrodite
    3. (c) Protandrous
