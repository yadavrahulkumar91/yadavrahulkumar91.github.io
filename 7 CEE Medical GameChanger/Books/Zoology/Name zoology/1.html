1. 1. Which of the following pairs have the same body plan and symmetry?
    1. (a) Obelia and starfish
    2. (b) Cockroach and frog
    3. (c) Hydra and earthworm
    4. (d) Pigeon and Leucosolenia
    (c) Hydra and earthworm have the same body plan and symmetry.
  2. 2. Schizocoel is the body cavity of
    1. (a) Mollusca
    2. (b) Echinoderms
    3. (c) Platyhelminthes
    4. (d) Annelids
    (d) Schizocoel is the body cavity of Annelids.
  3. 3. Starfish belong to
    1. (a) Mollusca
    2. (b) Echinodermata
    3. (c) Annelids
    4. (d) None
    (b) Starfish belong to the phylum Echinodermata.
  4. 4. Which one of the following is a true fish?
    1. (a) Devil fish
    2. (b) Cuttle fish
    3. (c) Silver fish
    4. (d) Flying fish
    (c) Silver fish is a true fish.
  5. 5. Salamander is
    1. (a) Reptile
    2. (b) Mammal
    3. (c) Amphibian
    4. (d) Bird
    (c) Salamander is an amphibian.
  6. 6. Coelom derived from blastocoel is known as
    1. (a) Pseudocoelom
    2. (b) Schizocoel
    3. (c) Haemocoel
    4. (d) Enterocoel
    (d) Coelom derived from blastocoel is known as Enterocoel.
  7. 7. Which of the following is the smallest unit of classification?
    1. (a) Phylum
    2. (b) Variety
    3. (c) Genus
    4. (d) Species
    (d) Species is the smallest unit of classification.
  8. 8. Neotype is
    1. (a) Nomenclature type from original material
    2. (b) Nomenclature type when the original is missing
    3. (c) One of the two or more specimens cited by author
    4. (d) New species discovered by scientist
    (b) Neotype is the nomenclature type when the original is missing.
  9. 9. Most primitive organisms are
    1. (a) Monerans
    2. (b) Protists
    3. (c) Fungi
    4. (d) Algae
    (a) Monerans are considered the most primitive organisms.
  10. 10. Complexity in classification is of
    1. (a) Prokaryotes
    2. (b) Unicellular eukaryotes
    3. (c) All animals
    4. (d) All plants
    (c) Complexity in classification is observed in all animals.
  11. 11. Which of the following category contains organisms least similar to one another?
    1. Class
    2. Genus
    3. Family
    4. Species
    (a) Class contains organisms least similar to one another.
  12. 12. Haemocoel is present in:
    1. Flatworms
    2. Roundworms
    3. Segmented worms
    4. Insects
    (d) Haemocoel is present in insects.
  13. 13. The excretion in birds and reptiles is:
    1. Aminotelic
    2. Ammonotelic
    3. Uricotelic
    4. Ureotelic
    (c) The excretion in birds and reptiles is uricotelic.
  14. 14. Gerontology is the study of:
    1. Regeneration
    2. Ageing
    3. Death
    4. Growth
    (b) Gerontology is the study of ageing.
  15. 15. Study of human race?
    1. Eugenics
    2. Phenology
    3. Entomology
    4. Ethnology
    (d) Ethnology is the study of human race.
  16. 16. Ichthyology is the study of:
    1. Fishes
    2. Reptiles
    3. Birds
    4. Mammals
    (a) Ichthyology is the study of fishes.
  17. 17. Animals in which nitrogen is predominantly excreted in the form of urea are:
    1. Ammonotelic
    2. Ureotelic
    3. Uricotelic
    4. None of the above
    (b) Animals in which nitrogen is predominantly excreted in the form of urea are ureotelic.
  18. 18. The term biology was coined by:
    1. Aristotle
    2. Theophrastus
    3. Lamarck and Treviranus
    4. Linnaeus
    (c) The term biology was coined by Lamarck and Treviranus.
