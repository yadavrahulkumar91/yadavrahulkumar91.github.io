1. 1. Incurrent canal is associated with:
    1. (a) Echinodermata
    2. (b) Porifera
    3. (c) Coelenterata
    4. (d) Protozoa
    (b) Incurrent canal is associated with Porifera.
  2. 2. Which one of the following is a class of Porifera?
    1. (a) Hydrozoa, Scyphozoa, Anthozoa
    2. (b) Calcarea, Demospongia, Hexactinellida
    3. (c) Scyphozoa, Cephalopoda Calcarea
    4. (d) Calcarea, Hydrozoa, Demospongia
    (b) The classes of Porifera are Calcarea, Demospongia, and Hexactinellida.
  3. 3. Classification of Porifera is based on:
    1. (a) Canal system
    2. (b) Reproduction
    3. (c) Amoebic cells
    4. (d) Spicules
    (a) The classification of Porifera is based on the canal system.
  4. 4. Choanocyte cells are found in:
    1. (a) Protozoa
    2. (b) Echinodermata
    3. (c) Porifera
    4. (d) Coelenterata
    (c) Choanocyte cells are found in Porifera.
  5. 5. The sponge differs from other animals by the presence of:
    1. (a) Collar cells
    2. (b) Hollow body
    3. (c) Coelenteron cavity
    4. (d) Only one mouth
    (a) The sponge differs from other animals by the presence of collar cells.
  6. 6. Gemmule formation occurs in:
    1. (a) Porifera
    2. (b) Coelenterata
    3. (c) Annelida
    4. (d) Echinodermata
    (a) Gemmule formation occurs in Porifera.
  7. 7. The invertebrate without neurons is:
    1. (a) Hydra
    2. (b) Sponges
    3. (c) Planaria
    4. (d) Earthworm
    (b) The invertebrate without neurons is Sponges.
  8. 8. Sponges differ from other metazoans in having:
    1. (a) Hollow body
    2. (b) Coelenteron
    3. (c) Collar cells
    4. (d) Tentacles
    (c) Sponges differ from other metazoans in having collar cells.
  9. 9. Amphiblastula is the larva of:
    1. (a) Leucosolenia
    2. (b) Sycon
    3. (c) Hydra
    4. (d) Planaria
    (a) Amphiblastula is the larva of Leucosolenia.
  10. 10. Gemmule formation is the means of reproduction in:
    1. (a) Porifera
    2. (b) Arthropoda
    3. (c) Coelenterata
    4. (d) Annelida
    (a) Gemmule formation is the means of reproduction in Porifera.
  11. 11. Which of the following is a freshwater sponge?
    1. (a) Sycon
    2. (b) Euspongia
    3. (c) Spongilla
    4. (d) Euplectella
    (c) Spongilla is a freshwater sponge.
  12. 12. What are the main cells of which poriferan outer walls are made?
    1. (a) Pinacocytes
    2. (b) Choanocytes
    3. (c) Porocytes
    4. (d) Amoebocytes
    (a) The main cells of which poriferan outer walls are made are pinacocytes.
