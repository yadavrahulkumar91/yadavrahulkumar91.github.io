1. The carrier of Trypanosoma in man is:
    1. (a) Tse-tse fly
    2. (b) Mosquito
    3. (c) Sand fly
    4. (d) House fly
    (a) The carrier of Trypanosoma in man is the Tse-tse fly.
  2. Holozoic type of nutrition is found in:
    1. (a) Protozoa
    2. (b) Fungi
    3. (c) Dinoflagellates
    4. (d) Algae
    (a) Holozoic type of nutrition is found in protozoa.
  3. Pseudopodia retain their movement due to a change in:
    1. (a) Viscosity
    2. (b) Pressure
    3. (c) Water movement
    4. (d) Pellicle
    (a) Pseudopodia retain their movement due to a change in viscosity.
  4. The disease caused by protozoa is:
    1. (a) Kala-azar
    2. (b) Typhoid
    3. (c) Cholera
    4. (d) Whooping cough
    (a) The disease caused by protozoa is Kala-azar.
  5. Kala-azar is transmitted by:
    1. (a) Bed bug
    2. (b) Sand fly (Phlebotomus argentipus)
    3. (c) Mosquito
    4. (d) Pediculus
    (b) Kala-azar is transmitted by the sand fly (Phlebotomus argentipus).
  6. Protozoan class that does not carry its name after its locomotory organ is:
    1. (a) Flagellate
    2. (b) Rhizopoda
    3. (c) Sporozoa
    4. (d) Ciliata
    (c) The protozoan class that does not carry its name after its locomotory organ is Sporozoa.
  7. Contractile vacuole of Amoeba and some other protozoans is related to:
    1. (a) Digestion
    2. (b) Food collection
    3. (c) Water absorption
    4. (d) Osmoregulation
    (d) The contractile vacuole of Amoeba and some other protozoans is related to osmoregulation.
  8. Entamoeba histolytica causes:
    1. (a) Sleeping sickness
    2. (b) Malaria
    3. (c) Kala-azar
    4. (d) Dysentery
    (d) Entamoeba histolytica causes dysentery.
  9. The infective stage of E. histolytica is:
    1. (a) Trophozoite
    2. (b) Precystic stage
    3. (c) Cystic stage
    4. (d) Tetranucleate cyst
    (c) The infective stage of E. histolytica is the cystic stage.
  10. Euglena is:
    1. (a) Ciliate
    2. (b) Sporozoan
    3. (c) Flagellate
    4. (d) Sarcodina
    (c) Euglena is a flagellate.
  11. 11. Amoeba capture its active prey by
    1. (a) Circumfluence
    2. (b) Circumvallation
    3. (c) Import
    4. (d) Invagination
    (b) Amoeba captures its active prey by circumvallation.
  12. 12. If an amoeba is placed in distilled water, its contractile vacuole
    1. (a) Works faster
    2. (b) Works slowly
    3. (c) Disappears
    4. (d) Divides
    (b) If an amoeba is placed in distilled water, its contractile vacuole works slowly.
  13. 13. An amoeba transferred from a container X to another container Y developed a contractile vacuole, but the vacuole disappeared again when the Amoeba was transferred back to container X. The container X and Y respectively contained.
    1. (a) Fresh and marine water
    2. (b) Marine and freshwater
    3. (c) Both contained freshwater
    4. (d) Both contained marine water
    (b) Container X contained marine water, and container Y contained freshwater.
  14. 14. When a freshwater pond is about to dry, the likely response of Amoeba will be
    1. (a) Encystment
    2. (b) Binary fission
    3. (c) No change
    4. (d) Quick feeding
    (a) When a freshwater pond is about to dry, Amoeba responds by undergoing encystment.
  15. 15. In unfavourable conditions, reproduction of Amoeba is by
    1. (a) Endomixis
    2. (b) Conjugation
    3. (c) Binary fission
    4. (d) Sporulation
    (d) In unfavourable conditions, reproduction of Amoeba occurs through sporulation.
  16. 16. Amoeba is called immortal and it is scattered all over the world due to the formation of
    1. (a) Spores
    2. (b) Regeneration
    3. (c) Binary fission
    4. (d) All of these
    (d) Amoeba is called immortal and it is scattered all over the world due to the formation of spores, regeneration, and binary fission.
  17. 17. Amoebulae are the products of
    1. (a) Syngamy
    2. (b) Fragmentation
    3. (c) Binary fission
    4. (d) Multiple fission
    (d) Amoebulae are the products of multiple fission.
  18. 18. Amoebic dysentery is caused by
    1. (a) Entamoeba histolytica
    2. (b) Amoeba proteus
    3. (c) Entamoeba coli
    4. (d) Entamoeba gingivalis
    (a) Amoebic dysentery is caused by Entamoeba histolytica.
  19. 19. Which of the following has only one host?
    1. (a) Entamoeba histolytica
    2. (b) Trypanosoma gambiense
    3. (c) Taenia solium
    4. (d) Plasmodium vivax
    (d) Plasmodium vivax has only one host.
  20. 20. The number of pseudopodia present in the trophozoite of Entamoeba histolytica is
    1. (a) One
    2. (b) Two
    3. (c) Many
    4. (d) None
    (c) The trophozoite of Entamoeba histolytica has many pseudopodia.
  21. 21. Entamoeba histolytica in man is found in:
    1. Colon
    2. Small intestine
    3. Oral cavity
    4. Stomach
    (a) Entamoeba histolytica in man is found in the colon.
  22. 22. How many metacystic uninucleate trophozoites are produced from a single infective cyst of Entamoeba histolytica?
    1. 8
    2. 1
    3. 4
    4. 2
    (a) 8 metacystic uninucleate trophozoites are produced from a single infective cyst of Entamoeba histolytica.
  23. 23. Infective stage of Entamoeba histolytica is:
    1. Trophozoite
    2. Precystic form
    3. Metacystic form
    4. Quadrinucleate cyst
    (c) The infective stage of Entamoeba histolytica is the metacystic form.
  24. 24. The disease caused by Entamoeba gingivalis is spread through:
    1. Anopheles
    2. Kissing
    3. Air
    4. Housefly
    (b) The disease caused by Entamoeba gingivalis is spread through kissing.
  25. 25. Reserve food in Euglena viridis is mainly:
    1. Fats
    2. Starch
    3. Paramylon
    4. Both b and c
    (c) The reserve food in Euglena viridis is mainly paramylon.
  26. 26. African sleeping sickness is caused by:
    1. Trypanosoma gambiense and transmitted by Glossina palpalis

  27. 27. Trypanosoma finally invades:

  28. 28. A person suspecting the infection of Trypanosoma should get examined:
    1. Urine
    2. Stool
    3. Blood smear
    4. Saliva

  29. 29. Dum dum fever is caused by:
    1. Plasmodium
    2. Leishmania

  30. 30. Sleeping sickness is caused by:
    1. Entamoeba
    2. Monocytes
    3. Trypanosoma
    4. E. coli
    (c) Sleeping sickness is caused by Trypanosoma.
  31. 31. The secondary host of Trypanosoma for man is:
    1. Glossina palpalis
    2. Glossina morsitans
    3. Phlebotomus
    4. None of these
    (a) The secondary host of Trypanosoma for man is Glossina palpalis.
  32. 32. Cell drinking may be technically expressed as:
    1. Pinocytosis
    2. Diffusion
    3. Phagocytosis
    4. Osmosis
