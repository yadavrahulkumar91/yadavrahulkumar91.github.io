ARTHROPODA

Definition and Characteristics

  1. Arthropoda: Meaning "Jointed Appendages"
  2. Voluntary Muscles Associated with Jointed Appendages

Diversity and Success

  1. Largest Phylum in Animal Kingdom
  2. In Terms of Taxa and Biomass
  3. Most Successful Animal Group
  4. Due to Chitinous Exoskeleton
    1. Cuticle Moulting (Larva) or Ecdysis (Adult)
    2. Secreted by Epidermis

General Features

  1. Bilaterally Symmetrical
  2. Triploblastic
  3. Segmented with Tagmatization
    1. Only Internal Segmentation
    2. Fixed Number of Segments
  4. Haemocoelomate
  5. Tube Within Tube Body Plan
    1. Complete Alimentary Canal
      1. Foregut
      2. Midgut
      3. Hindgut

Circulatory System

  1. Open Circulatory System
    1. No Blood Capillaries
    2. Blood Vessels Open in Sinus or Lacunae
  2. Dorsal Contractile Heart

Respiratory System

  1. Respiration by Various Structures
  2. Merostomata (Limulus): Book Gills
  3. Arachnida: Book Lungs
  4. Crustacea: Gills
  5. Myriopoda: Trachea
  6. Insecta: Trachea / Air Tubes

Excretory System

  1. Excretory Organs in Different Groups
  2. Merostomata: Not Specified
  3. Arachnida: Coxal Gland or Malpighian Tubules
  4. Crustacea: Antennary Gland or Green Gland
  5. Myriopoda: Urate Cells and Malpighian Tubules
  6. Insecta: Not Specified
  7. Major Excretory Product: Uric Acid

Nervous System

  1. Dorsal Nerve Ring
  2. Double Ventral Nerve Cord

Sense Organs

  1. Antennae
  2. Simple Eyes
  3. Compound Eyes
  4. Statocyst
  5. Chemoreceptors
  6. Taste Receptors

Reproduction

  1. Dioecious
  2. Internal Fertilization
  3. Majority Oviparous
  4. Some Ovoviviparous (Scorpions)
  5. Development: Direct or Indirect, Parthenogenesis in Some

Classification

  1. Onychophora
  2. Primitive Arthropods
  3. Commonly called "Velvet Worm" or "Walking Worms"
  4. Example: Peripatus (Connecting Link)
  5. Trilobitomorpha
  6. Fossil Arthropods
  7. Chelicerata
  8. Appendages: Chelicerae, Pedipalps
  9. Body: Prosoma, Ophistosoma
  10. Classes: Merostomata, Arachnida
  11. Mandibulata
  12. Body: Head, Thorax, Abdomen
  13. Presence of Antennae, Mandibles, Maxillae
  14. Classes: Crustacea, Myriopoda, Insecta
  15. Examples: Crab, Prawn, Water Flea, Crayfish, Shrimp, Sacculina
