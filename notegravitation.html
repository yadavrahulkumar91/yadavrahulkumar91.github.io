Physics Notes

A. Newton's Force of gravity

I. F=(Gm1 m2)/r2

where, G=6.67×10−11 Nm2 kg−2

B. Acceleration due to gravity (g)

I. Formula

  1. g=GM/r2

II. Variation of g

  1. Due to shape of earth
    • The value of g increases from equator to pole.
  2. At height h
    • g'1=(R/(R+h)2)g
    • g'2=(1−2hR)g (h<R)
  3. At depth d
    • g'3=(1−d/R)g
  4. Due to rotation of earth
    • g'4=g−ω2 R cos2λ

C. Gravitational field strength/ Gravitational field intensity/ Gravitational field

I. E⃗=GM/r2

D. Gravitational potential (V)

V=−GM/r

E. Gravitational potential energy (U)

U=−GMm/r

F. Potential difference △U=U−Uo=mgh(R/(R+h))

G. Satellite

I. Orbital velocity

  1. (mv2)/r=GMm/r2
  2. v=√(GM/r)

II. Velocity to put the satellite into orbit of radius r (1st cosmic velocity)

  1. (vo2)/rg=1

III. Escape velocity (2nd cosmic velocity)

  1. (ve2)/Rg=2

IV. 3rd cosmic velocity

Velocity to put the body out of the solar system.
