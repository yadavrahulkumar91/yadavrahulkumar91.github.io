Motion in a Straight Line

Learn the fundamental concepts of units and dimensions, and how to handle errors in measurements.

1. Definition of Motion in a Straight Line

If a body is in uniform motion (velocity = constant) or body has acceleration and velocity acting at 0° or 180° , then path followed is straight line and motion is Rectilinear motion.

2. Basic formulas

a. Distance and Displacement

Distance Displacement
Definition The length of path followed by a moving body is called distance. The Shortest distance between initial and final position with direction for a moving body is called displacement.

Relation with time For a moving body, distance always increases with time. For a moving body, displacement may increase or decrease with time.

Formula for Average Velocity

vav = (total displacement) / (total time)
