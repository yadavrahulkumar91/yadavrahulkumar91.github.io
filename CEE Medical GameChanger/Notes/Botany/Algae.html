Algae

Thallophyta are a diverse group of organisms that include everything from single-celled algae to complex multicellular seaweeds.

            Reproduction

            • Vegetative reproduction
              • By fragmentation
              • By Hormogonium formation: In BGA
                1. Either necrotic cell or Heterocyst takes part in fragmentation of trichome.
                2. Multicellular fragment of Trichome is called Hormogonium.
              • By cell division method; In unicellular algae.
            • Asexual reproduction:
              • Takes place by formation of Asexual spore "Mitospore".
              • Zoospore Aplanospore Hypnospore Akinetes
                • Motility: Flagella +ve for Zoospore
                • Covering: -ve for Zoospore, Thin for Aplanospore, Thick for Hypnospore, and Very thick for Akinetes
                • Produced in: Favourable condition for Zoospore and Aplanospore, Unfavourable condition for Hypnospore, and Highly unfavourable condition for Akinetes
                • Takes part in Perennation
                  • Perennation is a method of conserving from one season to another season.
