Hydrophytes

1. Free floating hydrophytes

  • Float freely on the surface of water but are not rooted in the soil.
  • Contact with both water and air
  • Examples:
    • Eichhornia
    • Pistia
    • Lemna
    • Wolffia

    Spongy petiole

2. Free floating submerged hydrophytes

  • Grow below the water surface and are not in contact with atmosphere.
  • Examples:
    • Ceratophyllum
    • Utricularia

Rooted Floating Hydrophytes

  • Rooted in the mud but their leaves and flowering shoots float on the water surface
  • Examples:
    • Trapa
    • Nelumbo
    • Nymphaea
    • Marsilea
    • Victoria
    • Spongy petioles

Rooted Submerged Hydrophytes

  • Completely immersed in water and rooted in the mud
  • Examples:
    • Hydrilla
    • Vallisneria

Emergent Hydrophytes/ Amphibious Hydrophytes

  • Grow in shallow water
  • Roots, some parts of stems and leaves are submerged in water. But, some flowering shoots spring well above the water surface.
  • Examples:
    • Limnophylla
    • Ranunculus
    • Sagittaria
    • Typha
